# Leaderboards

Leaderboard entries are organized by **Dataset × Task × Metric × Split**. Different protocols are not mixed. Metrics such as R@1, AP, SDM, meter-level distance, and pose error are not converted into each other.

Only benchmarks with publicly accessible datasets and clearly defined evaluation protocols are included in the official leaderboards. Datasets or protocols that are not publicly released, not reproducible, or only used as auxiliary transfer/robustness settings are listed as notes but not ranked.
