# SUES-200

SUES-200 is a multi-height, multi-scene cross-view image benchmark for drone and satellite geo-localization. It contains images captured at four drone flight altitudes (150m, 200m, 250m, 300m) with corresponding satellite tiles. The dataset is split into per-altitude training and test sets with disjoint location classes.

Only final flagship method results on the official dataset protocol are listed. Automatically reviewed rows are marked `verified=false` until manual checking.
