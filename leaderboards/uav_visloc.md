# UAV-VisLoc

UAV-VisLoc is a large-scale dataset for cross-view UAV geo-localization in GNSS-denied environments, containing 14,998 UAV images from 30 flight trajectories across six continents, paired with over 120,000 geo-referenced satellite tiles. The images span altitudes from 160 to 2,500 meters and include both RGB and infrared modalities from real and synthetic flights.

Only final flagship method results on the official dataset protocol are listed. Automatically reviewed rows are marked `verified=false` until manual checking.
